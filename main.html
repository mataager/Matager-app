Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch plans at any time?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged a prorated amount for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
Yes, all paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. You can test all the features of the plan without providing credit card information. After 14 days, you'll need to choose a plan to continue using the service.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. For Enterprise plans, we also accept purchase orders and can arrange custom payment terms.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or educational institutions?
Yes, we offer a 50% discount for registered non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Please contact our sales team with proof of your status to get the discount applied to your account.
What happens to my data if I cancel my subscription?
Your data is preserved for 30 days after cancellation, during which you can reactivate your account and retain all data. After 30 days, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers. We recommend exporting your data before cancellation if you wish to keep it.
